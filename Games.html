CrackJack

CrackJack is a blackjack 2D platformer game using unity and AR. Vuforia was very hard to work with throughout the project, specifically when trying to get it to read, understand, and incorporate the cards into the game. The UI was also difficult to get it to do what was intended. Regardless, through completing the project, I learned how to incorporate completely different tools into a game and function together. The best part of the project was shooting ideas off each group member and finding ways to work together to accomplish what we needed.
