Interactive Comic





  • The Comic file + other work


An Interactive comic titled “The Emptiness of an Atom” -a topic given to us by our client Dr. Ellertson. It is an immersive storytelling project created in unity using a lot of techniques, animations, and art from adobe animate and illustrator. As group lead in this project, I oversaw how and when all my group members did what they needed to do. The best part of this project was crafting the storyboard, getting rejected by our client, and then making it better.

Olympics Animation (rotoscopy)





The purpose of the animation is to promote the Olympics and get people excited while also possibly having a subliminal effect on their conscious. I learned that rotoscope can be an arduous process, and while it was very fun to make because the process made me excited, it was also stressful at times.

The Only Way to Slide in Baseball



This was my first project that I ever made, and I hold it very dear to my heart. This project taught me how much more I can learn, and it gave me inspiration for all of the projects since then. I had the time of my life making this project and I wouldn’t be where I am now without it.



Adobe XD -Art Gallery Phone App



  • Wireframe Example


This artifact is a demonstration of illustrations that I’ve made throughout my tenure at GIMM through a phone app created entirely in ADOBE XD. This project taught me how to adjust to a new program quickly and forced me to focus on innovating with only what’s in front of me. The fun part of this project was easily the interactivity that I learned can most definitely be used in a myriad of ways.
