Interactive Comic

(Demo Video On About/Home Page)

An Interactive comic following “The Emptiness of an Atom” a topic given to us by our client Dr. Ellertson. The interactive comic shows a visual pleasing novel of drawings and animations while following a story that explains the deeper meaning that is the emptiness of an Atom. The most important part of this project that I learned, was overseeing how and when all my group members did what they needed to do because I was group lead. The best part of this project was crafting the storyboard, getting rejected by our client, and then making it better.
